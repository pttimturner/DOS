About Us

Digital Office Solution's founder and Vice-President, Julio Reis, worked at Duplicating Specialties as a Service Technician in 1989. He worked his way up to Service Manager. After learning the business from his mentor, Mr. Robert Scoggins, Julio and his wife Fernanda bought Duplicating Specialties in 1998. In 2000 the name was changed to Digital Office Solutions and land was purchased to build the building DOS now occupies.



Julio's experience as a Service Technician and his time as the Service Manager keeps him and this company on target...THE CUSTOMER. Julio has built DOS on SERVICE. Being fair and keeping the customer happy is his number one concern.
